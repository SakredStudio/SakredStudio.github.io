Privacy Policy — Swaiyu

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Swaiyu (“the app,” “we,” “us”) is operated by Sakred Studio. This policy explains how Swaiyu handles information when you use it. Swaiyu is a K-pop fan companion app offering concert planning, release tracking, an AI fan assistant (FANI), style recommendations, and fan tools.

1. No Account, No Sign-Up.

Swaiyu does not require an account. We do not ask for or collect your name, email, phone number, location, or other personal identifiers to use the app.

2. Information Stored on Your Device.

Your in-app preferences — selected groups, wishlist, saved concert, fan card details, and checklist progress — are stored locally on your device via your browser’s local storage. This information stays on your device. It is not transmitted to us or any server, and we cannot access it. You can erase it anytime by clearing the app’s data in your device settings.

3. AI Assistant (FANI).

When you send a message to FANI, that message is transmitted over a secure (HTTPS) connection to our AI provider, Anthropic (the maker of Claude), through a Cloudflare Worker that routes the request. Anthropic processes the message to generate a response. These messages are not linked to any account or identity. We do not use them to build a profile of you. Please do not share sensitive personal information (financial, health, or government IDs) in FANI chats. Anthropic and Cloudflare process this data as our service providers, under their own terms and privacy policies.

4. Affiliate Links.

Swaiyu contains affiliate links to third-party retailers (e.g., ticket marketplaces and fashion stores), provided through affiliate networks including Sovrn. If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. The affiliate network and destination retailer may set cookies or use tracking subject to their own policies. We do not control these third-party sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

5. Analytics.

We do not use first-party analytics or advertising SDKs in Swaiyu. The only third-party tracking that may occur is click tracking by our affiliate network (Sovrn) when you follow an affiliate link.

6. Service Providers.

Swaiyu relies on: Anthropic (powers FANI), Cloudflare (routes FANI requests), Sovrn (affiliate links and click tracking), and GitHub Pages (hosting). Each operates under its own privacy policy.

7. Data Security.

Information stored by the app remains on your device. Requests to the AI assistant are sent over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. No system is completely secure, but we use measures appropriate to the limited data the app handles.

8. Children’s Privacy.

Swaiyu is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app collects no personal information and requires no account, no personal data about any user is gathered by us. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will address it.

9. Your Choices.

Because your data is stored locally, you control it — clear it anytime through your device settings. Questions about data sent to our AI provider can be directed to the contact below.

10. Changes to This Policy.

We may update this policy from time to time, reflected by the “Last updated” date above.

11. Contact.

Questions about this policy or Swaiyu’s data practices: Sakred Studio — sakredfamily@gmail.com
